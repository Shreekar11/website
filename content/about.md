👋🏻 Hi, I’m Shreekar.

I'm a software engineer based in Pune, building distributed systems and AI systems that are reliable, resilient, and production-ready.

I’m particularly interested in workflow + agents orchestration (Temporal), event-driven architectures, and designing systems that don’t break under real-world constraints.

## Work

I’ve worked across both 0 to 1 builds and end-to-end production systems.

At Atlan, I worked on distributed systems powering a Microsoft Teams Marketplace integration - building microservices, handling cross-cloud infra (AWS + Azure), and designing event-driven systems using Kafka. I also spent time making systems more reliable (and less memory-hungry).

At Attack Capital (YC W22), I built AI systems from scratch - including real-time voice AI interviewers and distributed backend systems with queues, workers, retries, and async pipelines.
